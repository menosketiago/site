Nem Tudo o que Reluz é Ouro or Not all That Shines is Gold is a portuguese saying akin to the more international "You can't judge a book by it's cover".

It is also the name of an handmade screenprinted fanzine in which I took part with an illustration, among many other great portuguese creatives.
